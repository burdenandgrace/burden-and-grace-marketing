Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Effective Date: April 15, 2026

Burden & Grace ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account or use the App, we may collect:

  • Email address (used to create and manage your account)
  • Password (stored securely and encrypted — we never see it in plain text)
  • Journal entries you write within the App
  • Devotional reading progress and completed days
  • Notification preferences (time and enabled/disabled status)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage data (which screens are visited, features used)
  • Crash reports and performance data to help us fix bugs
  • Subscription and purchase status (managed by RevenueCat)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Personalize your devotional experience and track your progress
  • Send daily notification reminders (only if you enable them)
  • Process and manage your subscription through RevenueCat
  • Improve the App by understanding how it is used
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only in the following limited circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party companies to operate the App:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (stores your account and journal data securely)
  • RevenueCat — subscription billing and management
  • Expo / React Native — app delivery platform

These providers are contractually required to protect your data and may only use it to perform services on our behalf.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Burden & Grace, our users, or the public.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase, which uses industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. We implement Row-Level Security so that only you can access your own journal entries and progress data. While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Journal Entries

Your journal entries are private. They are encrypted and stored securely in your personal account. We do not read, analyze, or share your journal content. Only you can access your own entries.

6. Push Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, the App schedules local notifications on your device. These notifications are generated on-device using your devotional content. We do not use third-party push notification services that track your location or behavior based on notifications.

7. Children's Privacy

Burden & Grace is designed for adults (18 years and older). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete that information.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Export your journal entries

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@burdenandgrace.com.

9. Data Retention

We retain your account information and journal entries for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the Effective Date at the top of this document and notify you through the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@burdenandgrace.com
  • Website: burdenandgrace.com
