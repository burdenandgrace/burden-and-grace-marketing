Questions people actually ask

A few honest answers.

Is anything really free?

Yes. The ten-day Silent Struggles track, the full Bible reader, the journal, verse highlighting, and bookmarking. Free, forever, for everyone. Create a free account in under a minute and you're in.

What's the difference between For Him, For Her, and Couples?

For Him gives you the men's library: ten 30-day tracks and daily devotionals written for the weights men carry. For Her gives you the women's library: ten 30-day tracks and daily devotionals written for the weights women carry, by a woman. Couples gives both libraries on two separate accounts (one subscription) so each spouse keeps their own private journal and progress.

How does the Couples tier keep journals private?

Two separate accounts on one subscription. Each person has their own login, their own encryption key, their own journal entries. Even on the same family iPad, signing into your account loads only your data. Your spouse cannot read your journal. We cannot read your journal. The encryption is end-to-end.

What happens when the 300 cap is reached?

Founding pricing goes away for new sign-ups in that tier. Members who joined as founding keep their price as long as they remain subscribed. Standard pricing becomes the default going forward: $3.99/mo or $3
